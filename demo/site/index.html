What you're seeing

  1. Set the channel. Drag SNR and pick a condition. Auto lets Sonde choose the waveform; Manual lets you force one.
  2. Sonde runs the real DSP. Your payload is modulated, pushed through a synthetic HF channel, then demodulated and decoded — entirely in WebAssembly.
  3. Press play. The waterfall sweeps, the packet console shows TX vs. decoded RX byte-by-byte, and the recon photo rebuilds from what actually survived.

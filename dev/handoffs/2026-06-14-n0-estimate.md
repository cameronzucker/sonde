## Design (Codex-converged, two rounds — `dev/codex-prompt-n0-{estimate,followup}.md`)

- `n0_thermal` = median(|freq[k]|²)/ln2 over unoccupied FFT bins.
- `var(e)` = pilot-noise propagation `((1-u)²+u²)·n0_thermal` + leave-one-out
  pilot curvature `(u(1-u))²·q` + signal-scaled floor `0.5·mean|H_pilot|²`.
- LLR clamp scales `2.0/n0_eff` (was fixed ±20 at n0=0.1).
- **Lesson:** the thermal-only first cut broke high-SNR fading (Codex's Q5
  channel-estimate-error caveat). The fading gate caught it; the `var(e)` term +
  floor fixed it. The win is gradual (~2-3 dB), pilot-density-limited at the
  between-pilot null blind spot.
